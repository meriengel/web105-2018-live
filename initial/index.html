Shotokan Karate

Why I study

I'm often asked if Karate is relevant in a world that has so much gun violence. I don't study Karate with the thought that I am going to disarm a gunman. I study Karate primarily for the health benefits, but also for the mental training. I don't think like a victim. I am aware of my surroundings and have a plan to protect myself.

Where I study

I study Karate at McHenry County College in the Gym on Wednesdays from 9:00 am till 11:00 am

Highlights of the class
